About computer

A computer is an electronic device that processes data and performs tasks based on instructions known as programs.
It accepts input through devices like a keyboard or mouse, processes the information using the central processing unit (CPU), and produces output through devices such as a monitor or printer.
A computer is made up of hardware, including physical components like memory and storage, and software, which controls its operations.
Common operating systems include Microsoft Windows, macOS, and Linux.
Computers are used in many fields such as education, healthcare, business, and communication, making them an essential part of everyday life in the modern world.
